Dannie's website


Here is Dannie's personal website, which contains her personal work for 2020-2024. Dannie is an artist from Fuzhou, Fujian.

I graduated from the Shanghai Academy of Visual Arts in 2024, specialising in design and curation, and is also an art critic. I currently live and work in Shanghai and my artistic practice is very broad, with concerns ranging from folk culture to Queer culture to imagining the future. Through playful ways of building ironic narratives, deconstructing visual symbols and recreating their creation, I explore larger issues such as consumerism, technology, tradition and politics. I believe that art is a force for freedom of expression and intellectual enlightenment, and my work aims to resonate with the viewer, evoking others to reflect on and explore social phenomena. I hope that my works will trigger the senses and thoughts of the audience and further promote social progress and cultural innovation.
